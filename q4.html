"Burning" is a critically acclaimed 2018 South Korean psychological mystery drama directed by Lee Chang-dong, based on Haruki Murakami's short story "Barn Burning." The film follows a young aspiring writer, Jong-su, who reunites with a childhood acquaintance, Hae-mi, and becomes entangled in a tense and ambiguous relationship with her and a mysterious, wealthy man named Ben. As jealousy and suspicion grow, the story slowly unravels into a haunting exploration of class divide, alienation, and hidden violence. With its slow-burning tension and symbolic storytelling, "Burning" leaves viewers questioning reality and intention, making it a powerful and enigmatic cinematic experience.
